Propofol
Sleepy Axolotl 🦎
Why the Sleepy Axolotl
Isopropyl "ears/gills" droop = bulky groups slow metabolism
Phenol "mouth" —OH = makes it water-soluble enough to inject
Benzene head = rapid blood-brain barrier crossing
Clinical Pearls
Rapid onset and emergence ("milk of amnesia")
Mild hemodynamic depression (watch BP on induction)
Propofol infusion syndrome risk with prolonged high-dose use
