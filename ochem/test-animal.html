CRNA Pharmacology • Visual Mnemonics • Real Structures
Propofol
Sleepy Axolotl 🦎
Why the Sleepy Axolotl
Isopropyl "ears/gills" droop = bulky groups slow metabolism
Phenol "mouth" —OH = makes it injectable
Benzene head = rapid BBB crossing
Clinical Pearls
Rapid onset and emergence ("milk of amnesia")
Mild hemodynamic depression — watch BP on induction
Propofol infusion syndrome risk with prolonged high doses
Ethanol
Goofy Balloon Dog 🐩
Why the Goofy Balloon Dog
Zigzag C–C chain = bendy neck
—OH = the silly nose that makes it an alcohol
Clinical Pearls
Reference molecule for all alcohols and CNS depression
Enhances GABA — same target as many anesthetics
Lidocaine
Focused Arrow 💉
Why the Focused Arrow
Aromatic ring = arrowhead piercing tissue
Amide shaft = long-lasting (resists plasma esterases)
Clinical Pearls
Amide local anesthetic — 1–2 hour duration
Also used as antiarrhythmic
Sevoflurane
Chaotic Broken-Rake Gremlin 🌬️
Why the Broken-Rake Gremlin
7 fluorine tines = wild, spiky hair
Low blood solubility = in fast, out fast
Clinical Pearls
Fastest, cleanest emergence
Great for pediatric mask inductions
Fentanyl
Stern Traffic-Light Guardian 🚦
Why the Traffic-Light Guardian
Two stacked phenyl rings = warning lights
Highly lipophilic = rapid brain entry
Clinical Pearls
100× more potent than morphine
Risk of chest wall rigidity and apnea
Succinylcholine
Strained Pull-Up Bar Creature 🏋️
Why the Pull-Up Bar Creature
Two quaternary N⁺ "hands" gripping the bar
Ester bonds = the grip slowly fails
Clinical Pearls
Fastest depolarizing NMB — ~8–12 min duration
Cannot be reversed — watch for hyperkalemia & MH
Morphine
Cozy Sleeping Caterpillar 🐛
Why the Cozy Sleeping Caterpillar
Fused rings = segmented body
Phenolic OH = soft pillow for the head
Slow and heavy — doesn't rush
Clinical Pearls
Slower onset than fentanyl due to more water solubility
M6G metabolite is more potent — caution in renal failure
