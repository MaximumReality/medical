Real Structure
Why the Balloon Dog
C–C zigzag = the goofy bendy neck.

Lidocaine
"The Arrow" — ring arrowhead + amide shaft + amine tip
Real Structure
Why the Arrow
Ring = arrowhead piercing into nerve tissue.
Amide shaft = the flight path (long-lasting, resists enzymes).
Amine tip = changes shape mid-flight to land the block.
Clinical
Amide bond = NOT cleaved by plasma esterases → lasts 1–2 hrs.
Also antiarrhythmic — same Na⁺ channel mechanism in cardiac muscle.

Why the Traffic Light

tap to reveal real structure →
Succinylcholine
"Pull-Up Bar" — symmetrical center + N⁺ grips on each end
Real Structure
Why the Pull-Up Bar
Perfectly symmetrical — you grip both N⁺ ends equally.
Ester bonds = grip strength fading as enzymes cleave them.
When both esters are cleaved, you drop. No choice.
Clinical
Cannot be reversed — wait for plasma esterases.
Watch for hyperkalemia, malignant hyperthermia trigger, pseudocholinesterase deficiency.

Morphine
"Napping Caterpillar" — 5 ring segments, head on OH pillow
Real Structure (simplified)
Why the Caterpillar
5 fused ring "segments" — the caterpillar body.
Phenolic OH = the pillow the head rests on.
Doesn't rush anywhere. Slow onset. Long sleep.
Clinical
More water-soluble than fentanyl → slower BBB crossing → slower onset.
M6G metabolite is MORE potent — accumulates in renal failure. Dangerous.
